# Inclusive

Presentation: <a> https://www.figma.com/deck/iwFcY3KHI24IjUVn4MDYas</a>
## About the Project

Inclusive is a platform designed to improve accessibility and communication between deaf individuals and organizations.
S
The project focuses on reducing communication barriers by providing tools that support career development, performance tracking, and inclusive feedback for deaf professionals.

It also helps managers better understand, evaluate, and communicate with deaf employees in a simple and accessible way.

---

## Problem

Many deaf individuals face significant challenges in the workplace due to:

- Communication barriers with managers and teams  
- Lack of accessible feedback tools  
- Difficulty in career development and performance tracking  
- Limited inclusion in professional environments  

---

## Solution

Inclusive provides a digital platform that:

- Enables structured and accessible communication  
- Supports career development planning for deaf users  
- Helps managers track performance and provide feedback  
- Bridges the gap between deaf professionals and companies  

---

## How It Works

- Users can create and manage their professional profiles  
- Managers can monitor progress and provide structured feedback  
- The platform simplifies communication using clear and direct language  
- Integration with accessibility tools improves usability  

---

## Accessibility

The platform integrates with VLibras, a free Brazilian government tool that translates Portuguese text into Brazilian Sign Language (Libras).

Note: Currently, translation is limited to Portuguese text, which is sufficient for an MVP.

---

## Status

This project is currently in early-stage development (MVP).

---

## Vision

We believe inclusion is not optional — it is essential.

Inclusive aims to create a more equitable job market where deaf professionals have the same opportunities to grow, communicate, and succeed.
